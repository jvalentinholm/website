# Decision 002 – Target Audiences

**Project:** Atlas  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Date:** 2026-07-06

---

# Purpose

This document defines the target audiences for the Circle X website and establishes their priority.

The order of priority influences:

- Information architecture
- Navigation
- Homepage content
- Calls-to-action
- Visual storytelling
- Tone of voice

Every design and content decision should primarily create value for the highest-priority audiences.

---

# Primary Audience

## Waste Owners

Examples:

- Municipalities
- Waste management companies
- Waste sorting facilities
- Industrial companies
- Producer Responsibility Organisations (PROs)

### Their challenge

They have plastic waste streams that cannot be recycled mechanically in an economically viable way.

They are looking for industrial recycling solutions that are commercially scalable and environmentally responsible.

### What they want to know

- Can Circle X process our plastic waste?
- Which waste streams are accepted?
- What happens to the material?
- Is the solution commercially viable?
- How do we start a dialogue?

### Website objective

Create confidence.

Generate commercial enquiries.

---

# Secondary Audience

## Future Employees

Examples:

- Process engineers
- Automation engineers
- Mechanical engineers
- Production operators
- Software developers
- Commercial profiles

### Their challenge

They want meaningful work with ambitious colleagues.

### What they want to know

- What kind of company is Circle X?
- What are we building?
- Is the technology exciting?
- Is the company growing?
- Can I make a difference?

### Website objective

Position Circle X as one of the most attractive industrial technology companies in Northern Europe.

---

# Third Audience

## Investors

Examples:

- Venture Capital
- Infrastructure Funds
- Family Offices
- Strategic Investors
- Government-backed financiers

### What they want to know

- Is the business scalable?
- Is the technology differentiated?
- Is there commercial traction?
- Is the management team credible?
- Why will Circle X win?

### Website objective

Support investor confidence.

The website should not replace an investor presentation.

It should encourage investors to continue the dialogue.

---

# Fourth Audience

## Authorities

Examples:

- Environmental authorities
- Municipalities
- EU institutions
- Certification bodies

### Website objective

Demonstrate professionalism, compliance and transparency.

---

# Fifth Audience

## Press

### Website objective

Provide facts.

Provide news.

Provide contact information.

---

# Strategic Principle

The website is primarily:

- a commercial platform
- an employer branding platform

It is secondarily:

- an investor communication platform

The website should therefore be designed around customer and employee journeys rather than investor presentations.

---

# Success Criteria

Every visitor should quickly understand:

- whether Circle X is relevant to them
- where to continue
- how to contact Circle X
