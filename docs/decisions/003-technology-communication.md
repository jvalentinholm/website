# Purpose

This document defines how Circle X communicates across the website.

It ensures that every page, headline and call-to-action supports the same strategic positioning.

---

# Communication Objective

The purpose of the website is to:

- generate commercial dialogue with waste owners
- attract talented employees
- build confidence among investors
- demonstrate professionalism towards authorities
- support media enquiries

The website is not intended to replace technical documentation, grant applications or investor presentations.

---

# Target Audience Priority

1. Waste owners

2. Future employees

3. Investors

4. Authorities

5. Press

---

# Brand Position

Circle X is an industrial platform for chemical recycling.

The company converts difficult plastic waste into certified circular synthetic oil used as feedstock for the production of new plastics.

The platform—not the individual technology—is the primary story.

---

# Tone of Voice

Circle X communicates with:

- confidence
- clarity
- precision
- professionalism
- optimism

The tone should always feel industrial, factual and trustworthy.

Never exaggerated.

Never sensational.

---

# Writing Principles

## Explain value before technology

Start with the customer's problem.

Only then explain the technology.

---

## Commercial before technical

Describe why the solution matters before describing how it works.

---

## Evidence before claims

Support statements with:

- data
- certifications
- commercial agreements
- documented performance

Avoid unsupported marketing language.

---

## Explain. Don't impress.

Use simple language whenever possible.

Industrial buyers value clarity over complexity.

---

## Protect confidential know-how

Explain what the technology delivers.

Avoid explaining proprietary engineering solutions.

Never disclose information that may compromise existing or future patent applications.

# How We Describe the Technology

We explain:

- the problem
- the input
- the output
- the industrial value
- the environmental value

We avoid explaining:

- reactor design
- process control
- proprietary mechanics
- engineering details
- patent-sensitive information

Principle:

Explain the value.
Protect the know-how.

---

# Sustainability Communication

Avoid broad sustainability claims.

Instead communicate:

- circularity
- resource efficiency
- reduced dependence on virgin fossil feedstock
- chemical recycling
- documented environmental performance

Support statements with facts whenever possible.

---

# Recruitment Communication

Future employees should experience Circle X as:

- ambitious
- engineering-driven
- collaborative
- international
- industrial
- mission-driven

The website should inspire people to help build a new industrial category.

---

# Investor Communication

The website should demonstrate:

- commercial maturity
- industrial credibility
- scalability
- traction
- execution capability

The website should encourage further dialogue rather than replace an investor presentation.

---

# Calls to Action

Prefer:

Discuss your plastic waste stream

Explore accepted feedstock

Talk to our team

Join Circle X

Learn how it works

Avoid generic calls-to-action such as:

Read more

Click here

Contact us

---

# Language

All external communication is written in British English.

The language should feel natural to a European industrial audience.

---

# Success Criteria

Every visitor should leave the website with three impressions:

Circle X is credible.

Circle X is industrial.

Circle X is building something important.
